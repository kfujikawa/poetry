Particles To Breathe/ 19 Nov

Particles in the air meant for your lungs
Molecules that segregate and braise in cellular division
There's an uprising now
This steam a swell that comes in sets for you to ride with me
Waters melt and rise
Unbalanced and crazy Men who decide our fate

I hold this hovering
An ember against the wind
Of roar
Palms read against this unflinching tide

A culture that regurgitates itself for you
An owl pellet scream coughed up in the dead of night
Moonlight reflection
Lives used and lost

This timbre pulsing is a battlefield
An echo that becomes less and less

I'm a champion with no oversight and it's weary
I'm lost without a cause
You are my cause and so far away

My blood drips and rips for you all over torn pages discarded
Pages weakened by the sun and disuse
I can only ever be one way
In one house
In one color

You think this color is beautiful
Or is nothing

I mean to you only what you feel in the moment you read this
