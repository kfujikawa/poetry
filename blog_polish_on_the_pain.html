The Polish on the Pain/ 01 Nov

I have crushes
I feel lost because I feel unprepared
Like a child touching a hot stove because they don't know better
But I do know better
So, what to do?
My blisters find solace in the knowing at least
Knowing I'm too fucking much for you

You feel these tentacles of the birth of something
Synapses that grow before everyone's "can't"
Connections that form before hands are coated with ignorance
I find myself some sort of spectator or specter of a tornado of consequence
Can I inform the ravage of wind and casualty?
Can anyone really understand what it is to be with me if they haven't already felt the storm?

All tragedy before
Dipping in cold rivers
Seeing history planted before me
Learning about you so I can forget

Healing now is a simultaneous birth and loss
What to banish
You died me in a inch of water
I begged you for this knowledge of color and now it's a prism I can't keep straight
So many colors from so many women
They looked into my eyes and spilled secrets like blood
Lies like warm rain
Entering into my veins making my cells starved of oxygen
