Pressure/ 04 Nov

Posted by Kinuyo / Relationships

More than you can bear
Like the noise of a fire engine that makes you cringe
Like the pulling of the sun on your skin mixed with salt in the heat of the day

We wait in lines to get lost in the humanity
Maybe even paparazzi don't see us holding hands
I field questions and smile for YouTube and it's all avarice

The only truth I find in the uppercut of my lip against yours
My only reality dipping my head back in the headlines of your clavicle
A phantom

An Oculus projection that coasts my eyes
The refraction of the echo of you deep into my cornea
