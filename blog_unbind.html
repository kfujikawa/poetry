Unbind/ 05 Sept

I find some lost path
Tasting the stardust like snowflakes on my tongue in the dark
It lights my way
As I swallow it whole

Feral and existing between panes of stained glass
I open my mouth wide and ricochet a prayer of the Now
Holy deference I give to weeping grass that is called dew
Burning air that is called sunset
The plundering of a well in me that holds all the fire I can unleash with a kiss

I burn up all that is in my path
I wait for the ash to spring something forth like Athena
All the wisdom and grating of quills in ink
I hold my lines tight for you
I bind them in the rake of words across this page and in ribbons of breath
Beautiful and desperate to be tasted
Unbind me whole again
