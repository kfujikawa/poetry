Arrows Of Connection/ 14 Aug

I shoot out an arrow of connection
With words
With energy
Do we feel less alone?
As our hands are bound together
Buried in earth where we can feel the same pulse
The same song
Are these words a salve?
Are they a weapon?

Somehow pain imbued in our skin
Like a needle carrying the ink of our Fathers
Of our ancestors
Of their mistakes carved into our choices
Dragging blood behind them with their wet signature

I feel the tip of the knife of history on my tongue
Against my fangs
I push out poison
And I swallow it deep as well

Your breath ignites the gunpowder on all of my scars
And builds a new cosmos
A new law for gravity
I wonder if I missed your pull
I wonder how laws can be broken
Like the surface tension of tears on water
