The Dawn/ 06 Nov

Dawn brings death
All whispered lies and promises interchanged
A slight of hand magician spinning around a lens focused on yourself
A cruel child burning bugs through a magnifying glass
You singe your strategy into the beat of unsuspecting hearts

Your art is only a lure
A dead carcass for maggots
The unevolved feed on narcissistic flesh
And you in turn consume them

You wind your hands in wringing sinew
Your gaping mouth cuts tendons and muscle
A black hole never satisfied
Sucking marrow from bone
Sucking gravity from planets
Draining blood from the pulse of honor
Always hungry and craving novelty and praise

Like a fire all you do is consume
As your sad victims gasp for breath and choke on particles of ash
All you will ever do is burn
All you can do is eat to survive
Like a cancer until there is no host left
