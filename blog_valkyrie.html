Valkyrie/ 26 Dec

Hummingbirds wings vibrate air like hurricanes on repeat
A Valkyrie
A warrior that carries the dead to their end
A pattern that folds and repeats like Escher

Raise me like the dead
Speak me like a promise
I unfold for you
Origami creases unbend and crack in bone chilling cold
Tiny fractures of ice that melt against the deafening sound of my breath
A shot in the silent night

Some forward thinking movement
Some passionate daydream

I feel diluted in you like snowflakes in water
Like salinity in every ocean

If I stretch the muscles of my neck tight and howl
Can I shoot light like an arrow through the sinew?
Can I scrape the marrow from bone and light it on fire?
As stars break in on themselves

I blow my voice against the tide
I hit my Sirens against rock
I find a shatter of light
Welding this heat to you

There's a reckoning coming
A darkness that's all I can see
White horses ooze against my lips
Prophecies block my esophagus
Madness blooming like fire

I wait for you in the pocket of this
A clear patch of sky
A warm dry vacuum where I can still feel the light of stars bouncing off of my skin
I wait for you and hold against defeat
Because I know kingdoms will fall but you will come for me
