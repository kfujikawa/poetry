Gravity/ 08 Nov

Sadness is whiplash
The rocket of my brain against a wall of bone
Tendons pull and detach
I spin without gravity
And I need gravity in words, in actions

I rip against the sky of galaxy with poems and songs
My lungs exist in the vacuum of deafening pressure
I can't scream so I sing
Rocks skip on the surface and never touch the hollow, the thought, the need
I elect no safety net because anyone who cares will be dead long before me

I ride fast and take risks
I love hard and won't apologize

You swim in this ocean of negatives
Refractions of color of things that never really existed
I can't be happy here with you in this

I'm not special
Not some bullet proof snowflake
Not anyone's hero
Not a drooling admirer because my definition of beauty lives outside of convention
But I champion my cause all the same

I might be someone's wish
Pulled tight and released; I need to be your arrow
Still at the end of this suffering and beauty
I would rather die alone with my list all crossed off
Than slake my thirst with poison

Still
Still
I exist in viscous solution
In my own gravity
